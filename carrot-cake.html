Carrot Cake Recipe

It has carrots, so it's healthy...right?

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 100g Butter
  • 250g Self-Raising Flour
  • 300g Sugar
  • 250g grated carrots
  • 2 tsp cinnamon

Recipe

  1. Heat the oven to 180 degrees celcius.
  2. Whisk all the wet ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Mix together the dry ingredients in a seperate bowl.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour your mixture into the bakine tin. Remember to grease the tin first
  6. Bake for 30 minutes

Enjoy!
