Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

It has carrots, so it's healthy...right?

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 150g Butter
  • 250g Plain Flour
  • 150g Sugar
  • 200g Chocolate Chips
  • 2 tsp Vanila Extract

Recipe

  1. Heat the oven to 180 degrees celcius.
  2. Mix butter and sugar together until creamy.
  3. Mix in the egg and vanila extract.
  4. Mix in the flour followed by the chocolate chips.
  5. Make small balls of your cookie mix and place on a baking tray.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes

Enjoy!
