Apple Pie Recipe

The perfect dessert for autumn!

Ingredients

For the Filling

  • 1kg Apples
  • 3 Tbs Flour
  • 150g Sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon

For the Crust

  • 2 Eggs
  • 230g Butter
  • 350g Flour
  • 50g Sugar

Recipe

  1. Peel and cut the apples and leave for later.
  2. For the pastry, mix the butter and sugar until mixed.
  3. Mix in the eggs.
  4. Stir in the flour.
  5. Knead the dough into a ball and chall for two hours.
  6. Heat oven to 200 degrees celcius
  7. Roll the dough to cover your tin.
  8. Mix in your sugar and cinamon in a separate bowl.
  9. Coat your apples in the sugar mix.
  10. Put the apples in the tin and cover with leftover dough.
  11. Bake for 2 hours.

Enjoy!
