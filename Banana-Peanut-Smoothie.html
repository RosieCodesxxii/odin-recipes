The Best Banana and Peanut Smoothie Recipe

Here if one of my alltime favourite smooties! Perfect for breakfast or a snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 tbs Peanut Butter
  • 250ml Oat Milk
  • 50g oats

How to Make

  1. Cut up your banana and put in you blender
  2. Add the peanut butter and oats to your banana in your blender
  3. Pour your oat milk into the belnder
  4. Blend for two minute

Enjoy!
