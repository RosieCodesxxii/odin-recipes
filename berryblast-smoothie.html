Berry Blast Smoothie Recipe

Try the fresh smoothie to brighten your morning

Ingredients

  • 50g Blueberries
  • 50g Raspberries
  • 50g Strawberries
  • 50g Blackberries
  • 200ml Coconut Milk

Recipe

  1. Put all of your fruit in your blender.
  2. Add your coconut milk.
  3. Blend for two minutes

Enjoy!
